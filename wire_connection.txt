4. ⚡ Voltage Measurement

If the fan is generating a small DC voltage (e.g., 0–5V), connect one terminal to GND, and the other to A1 to read voltage:

    Fan positive ➜ A1

    Fan negative ➜ GND

Make sure voltage doesn’t exceed 5V!
